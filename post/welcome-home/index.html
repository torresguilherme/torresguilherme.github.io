Welcome Home

Published: Friday, Aug 16, 2019   |   2 minute read   |   Using 411 words

    tags: meta

    categories: en-us

I’ve been thinking about starting this website for some time now. To make myself a place where I can dump what I think about tech, programming, music, and whatever I’m doing in my spare time.

I’m not sure if you ended up here because I sent you a link related to this website, which is the most likely, or someone else did, or you’ve just been stalking me and found this. Whatever it was, welcome to my personal little corner. This is what I’m currently using as my online curriculum as well, so, if you want to contact me for professional or academic reasons, I also got you covered. And I hope the stuff I have to say light a little bit of interest in your mind as well.

The topics I will cover here, especially in the computer graphics and vision topics, are not for the faint of heart and maybe you won’t be able to understand everything I say in a few minutes. Don’t be discouraged by that, I also didn’t learn all of that in real time, so to speak. Some things will require you a little bit of university-level mathematics to understand, like linear algebra, analytic geometry and calculus with one or more variables, and I won’t teach that because, first of all, I don’t think I am the right person to do it, and there is plenty of material around the internet where you can learn this.

What I intend is to produce material which I believe it’s lacking, especially in Portuguese, which is my mother tongue. I see plenty of material on how to print “Hello world” in over 50 programming languages, or on how to draw a triangle in OpenGL, or good programming practices, or hell, whether you should enroll in a computer science major or not. I probably won’t touch these topics here since I noticed they’ve been overcovered already. Instead, I’ll do what I think I do well and this world needs, as smug as that may sound.

I won’t post here that frequently as well, some months I may not post at all. That doesn’t mean I died or stopped supporting my own blog, it just means that I have other priorities aside from writing here.

And I won’t make this much longer because if I do no one will read this, so, that’s it. Suit yourself. And if you have any feedback, questions or requests, feel free to contact me.
