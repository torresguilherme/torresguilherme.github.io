About me

   |   2 minute read   |   Using 298 words

I’m a computer science student, independent game developer and music composer from Brazil.

Born in March 18th, 1997.

Currently studying Computer Science at the Federal University of Minas Gerais, and will probably graduate in 2020.

What I know

I’ve been trained in algorithms and data structures since basic ordering stuff to linear and non-linear programming, concurrent programming, heuristic algorithms for optimizations of all kinds of functions, exact exponential algorithms and parametrized complexity. I also have a solid knowledge of computer architecture, especially CPUs, operational systems, databases, compilers and both structured and object oriented programming.

My main focus is on computer graphics and computer vision, and at the moment I’m doing research in inverse rendering and re-lighting at the Computer Vision and Robotics Laboratory of my university, advised by Prof. Erickson Rangel do Nascimento. I also have a long history in game development, which you can see more about in the “My arts and crafts” section.

Technologies I’m familiar with

  • C, C++, Rust, Python, Node.js
  • STL structures and functions for C++ for concurrency
  • Modern OpenGL programming in both C, C++ and PyOpenGL
  • Vulkan programming
  • OpenCV for image processing, computer vision (homography, pose estimation and augmented reality)
  • Tensorflow and Keras for machine learning with focus on CNNs for image and video processing

Professional and academic experience

  • 2016-2017: student/researcher at the Operational Research laboratory, at the Federal University of Minas Gerais, with a focus on heuristic algorithms for graph coloring, advised by Prof. Thiago Noronha.
  • 2018-2018: Back-end web developer at Rethink in Belo Horizonte, Brazil, with a focus on building APIs with Node.js and Java, and testing.
  • 2019-2019: Web developer at Keepsporting, Italy.
  • 2019-current: student/researcher at the Computer Vision and Robotics Laboratory at the Federal University of Minas Gerais.

Languages

I speak native Portuguese, fluent English, advanced Spanish and basic Italian.
